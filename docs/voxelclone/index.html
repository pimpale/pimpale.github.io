Instructions (Click to toggle)
  • Use mouse to look around
  • WASD controls to move.
    • (when flying) Space and Shift controls to move up and down vertically.
    • (when walking) Space to jump.
  • m to switch between walking and flying.
  • f to toggle fast mode.
  • Left Click and hold to break blocks.
  • Right Click and hold to place blocks.
  • Press keys 1-5 to select which block to place.
